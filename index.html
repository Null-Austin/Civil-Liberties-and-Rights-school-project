Civil Liberties and Civil Rights, what is the Difference?

Civil Liberties Civil Rights
Protection from the government
Protection from discrimination
Connected to the bill of rights
Applies to just the government
Applies to everyone

Job Example

A good example between the two, is a job application. When your job application is denied due to discrimination, That would be a example of Civil rights.

When you post that onto your story or wherever, that would be freedom of speech under your civil liberties.

More examples

Civil Liberties

Being able to report on any topic as a reporter


Being allowed to peacefully protest


Being able to practice any religion


Civil Rights

Being able to be hired to a job despite being a minority


Having the ability to vote


Having the same access as anyone else to public buildings
